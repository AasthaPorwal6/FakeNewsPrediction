What is Fake News Prediction?

Fake news prediction is the process of using machine learning and natural language processing techniques to determine the authenticity of news articles or reports. With the proliferation of misinformation and fake news on the internet, fake news prediction systems aim to identify and flag potentially deceptive or misleading content.

These systems typically analyze various features of news articles, such as the language used, the sources cited, and the context of the information, to assess the likelihood that the content is fabricated or misleading.

By detecting fake news early, these prediction systems can help mitigate the spread of misinformation, promote media literacy, and protect individuals from being misled by false information.
